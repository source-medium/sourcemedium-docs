## Recently Shipped

Recent releases that are now available to all customers:

- **Improved Visualizations** - Clearer charts with better labels and smarter formatting
- **Thread Continuity** - Follow-up questions maintain context within the same Slack thread
- **Multi-Store Scoping** - Each thread is scoped to one store for multi-store tenants

---

## Available Now

Ask the AI Analyst questions in Slack and get instant answers backed by your SourceMedium data.

**Core Capabilities:**

- **Ad-hoc questions** - From simple queries ("What was revenue last week?") to complex multi-angle analysis
- **Deep Analysis Mode** - Automatically engages for complex questions, exploring multiple perspectives before synthesizing a comprehensive answer
- **Definitions & schema** - Ask "What is LTV?" or "Which table has order data?"
- **Data diagnostics** - Run Data Health and Attribution Health checks on demand
- **Thread follow-ups** - Continue analysis in the same Slack thread (mention `@SourceMedium` in channels to keep context)

## On the Horizon

Longer-term capabilities in the pipeline:

- **Proactive Monitoring** - Get alerted to significant changes without asking ("Your Meta ROAS dropped 15% this week")
