## Overview

With the SourceMedium Managed Data Warehouse at your disposal, you unlock a range of possibilities for data analysis and visualization. 
Our platform is engineered to be compatible with a wide array of BI tools, allowing you to choose the one that best fits your team's 
skills and your organization's needs. Whether you prefer an industry giant or a niche player in the BI space, 
connecting to your Managed Data Warehouse is straightforward, empowering you to start developing insights with the tools you love.

## Supported BI Templates

### Looker Studio

We currently only offer specialized support and templates for Looker Studio, a popular choice for its rich visualizations and intuitive 
interface.

See instructions for how to leverage these templates [here](/data-activation/template-resources/looker-studio-template-copy-instructions).
