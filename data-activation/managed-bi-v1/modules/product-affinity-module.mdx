#### What is the Product Affinity module and what data can be found there?
The Product Affinity module pulls data from your commerce store(s) and reports on products that are frequently purchased together.
#### Common Questions & Insights That Can Be Answered Here:
<AccordionGroup>
<Accordion title="Where can I find which products are most often purchased together?">
This can be found in the **Product Affinity - Frequently Purchased Together (Top 50 Combos)** chart.
</Accordion>
<Accordion title="Where can I find which products are most often purchased after other products?">
This can be found in the **First to Second Order Affinity (Top 50 Combos)** chart.
</Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
