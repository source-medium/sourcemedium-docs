#### What is the Google Search Console module and what data can be found there?
This module directly connects to your Google Search Console. The data seen in this module does not enter into the Source Medium data model and is not available outside this module.


#### Common Questions & Insights That Can Be Answered Here:
<AccordionGroup>
<Accordion title="Where can I find our top landing pages by clicks and conversions?">
This can be found in the **Landing Pages Performance** table.

</Accordion>


<Accordion title="Where can I find the top queries that have generated the most clicks on my site?">
This can be found in the **Queries Performance** table.


</Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

#### Potential Reporting Differences & Discrepancies
This is a direct connection to your Google Search Console and the data does not enter into our data model.
