#### What is the Google Ads module and what data can be found there?
   - Direct connection to your Google Ads account, bypassing the SourceMedium data model. However, this data is still incorporated into the Marketing Overview and Executive Summary.
   - Due to the direct connection, conversions will align with the definition as set in the Google Ads account. For the Marketing Overview and Executive Summary, conversions are shown as 'purchase', 'sale', or 'subscription' (because SourceMedium ingest the raw data and can define conversions)

#### Common Questions & Insights That Can Be Answered Here:
<AccordionGroup>
<Accordion title="Where can I find my Google Ads cost? Conversions?">
These metrics can be found in the **Performance by Date** table.

</Accordion>
<Accordion title="Where can I find which of my Google Ads campaign types or specific campaigns are preforming best?">
This can be found in the **Performance by Campaign/Ad Group/Ad ID** chart.


</Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
