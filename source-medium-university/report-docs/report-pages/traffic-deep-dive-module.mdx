#### What is the Google Analytics 4 Traffic module and what data can be found there?
- This module reports live Traffic as reported by Google Analytics 4 (GA4).
- GA4 data is still incorporated through our data model into the Marketing Overview and Executive Summary. For more information about differences between GA4 and UA, see [Google's Documentation](https://support.google.com/analytics/answer/11986666?hl=en#zippy=%2Cin-this-article).

#### Common Questions & Insights That Can Be Answered Here:
<AccordionGroup>
<Accordion title="Where can I find which channel groupings are performing best?">
This can be found in the **Sessions by Channel Grouping** chart.

</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Where can I find Mobile engagement vs. Desktop engagement?">
This can be found in the **Sessions by Device** chart.

</Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

#### Potential Reporting Differences & Discrepancies
Revenue is as reported by GA4 and will not match Shopify-reported or marketing platform-reported revenue elsewhere in the dashboard.
