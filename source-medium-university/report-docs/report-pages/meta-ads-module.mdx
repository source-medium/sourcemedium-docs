#### Common Questions & Insights That Can Be Answered Here:
<AccordionGroup>
<Accordion title="Where can I find my Meta ads Revenue? CPO? CVR?">

</Accordion>

#### Potential Reporting Differences & Discrepancies
SourceMedium reports strictly on 7-day click attribution so if your Meta attribution window is set to something else, it will look like there is a discrepancy in the Meta reporting vs. SourceMedium dashboard.
