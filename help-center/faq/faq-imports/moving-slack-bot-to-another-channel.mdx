---
title: "How to move your Slack report bot to another Slack channel"
icon: 'question-mark'
---

1. Visit the Apps section of your Shopify store and Select **SourceMedium - Slack Reports**.
    
    ![](/images/article-imgs/moving-slack-bot-to-another-channel/slack1.png)
    
2. Select the **Add to Slack** button. This will allow you to re-install it into another channel within your Slack. We recommend installing in a channel dedicated to the report to decrease the noise and the traffic of any active discussion channel.
    
    ![](/images/article-imgs/moving-slack-bot-to-another-channel/slack2.jpg)
