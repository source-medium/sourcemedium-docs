## Follow this integration guide to connect your Twitter Ads data to SourceMedium.

### Requirements

- **Admin access** for Twitter Ads to be able to edit access to account

### Steps

1. Navigate to your account access screen
    1. Hover over your username in the Twitter Ads admin
    2. Click **Edit access to account**
    
    ![](/images/article-imgs/twitter-integration/Untitled.png)
    
2. Grant SourceMedium access to your account
    1. Click **Add access**
        
        ![](/images/article-imgs/twitter-integration/Untitled1.png)
        
    2. Add [@sm_integrations](http://twitter.com/sm_integrations) as a **Campaign analyst >** click **Save changes**
        
        ![](/images/article-imgs/twitter-integration/Untitled_2.png)
        
    3. The following message should appear if your changes are saved successfully
        
        ![](/images/article-imgs/twitter-integration/Untitled3.png)
