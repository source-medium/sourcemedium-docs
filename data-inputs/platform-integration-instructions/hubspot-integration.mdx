## Follow this integration guide to connect your HubSpot data to SourceMedium.

### Requirements

- **Admin access** to your Hubspot account to connect CRM or Marketing products
- **Sales Admin** access to connect to Sales products
- **Super Admin** access to replicate email events

To lean more about HubSpot user roles and permissions, please visit their [HubSpot documentation](https://knowledge.hubspot.com/articles/kcs_article/settings/hubspot-user-roles-guide)

### Steps

1. Grant SourceMedium **super admin** access
    1. Log into HubSpot
    2. Navigate to **Settings** > **Users & Teams**
    3. Click **Create user** on the upper left
    
    ![](/images/article-imgs/hubspot-integration/Untitled.png)
    
2. Invite [integrations@sourcemedium.com](mailto:integrations@sourcemedium.com) as a **super admin**
    
    ![](/images/article-imgs/hubspot-integration/Untitled1.png)
    
3. After the integration is complete, **remove** **[integrations@sourcemedium.com](mailto:integrations@sourcemedium.com)** **as a super admin**
    
    ![](/images/article-imgs/hubspot-integration/Untitled2.png)
