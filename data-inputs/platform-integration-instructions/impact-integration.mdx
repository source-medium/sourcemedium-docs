## Follow this integration guide to connect your Impact Radius data to SourceMedium.

### **Requirements**

- **Admin access** to your Impact Radius account

### Steps

1. Find your impact account **Account SID** and **Auth Token** to share with SourceMedium
    1. Login to your Impact console
    2. Click on the **vertical ellipsis** in the lower-left corner
    3. Click **Settings**
    4. On the settings page, locate the Technical section and click **API**
    
    ![](/images/article-imgs/impact-integration/Untitled.png)
    
2. Email the read-only copy of your **Account SID** and **Auth Token** credentials with SourceMedium to  **[integrations@sourcemedium.com](mailto:integrations@sourcemedium.com)**
    
    ![](/images/article-imgs/impact-integration/Untitled1.png)
    
    ### **Additional information**
    
    - [**Impact's REST API**](https://impact.com/partnerships/get-connected-with-rest-api/)
