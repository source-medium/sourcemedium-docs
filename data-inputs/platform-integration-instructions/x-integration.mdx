## Follow this integration guide to connect your X Ads data to SourceMedium.

### Requirements

- **Admin access** for X Ads to be able to edit access to account

### Steps

1. Navigate to your account access screen
    1. Hover over your username in the X Ads admin
    2. Click **Edit access to account**
    
    ![](/images/article-imgs/X-integration/Untitled.png)
    
2. Grant SourceMedium access to your account
    1. Click **Add access**
        
        ![](/images/article-imgs/x-integration/Untitled1.png)
        
    2. Add [@sm_integrations](http://x.com/sm_integrations) as a **Ad manager >** click **Save changes**
        
        ![](/images/article-imgs/x-integration/Untitled2.png)
        
    3. The following message should appear if your changes are saved successfully
        
        ![](/images/article-imgs/x-integration/Untitled3.png)
