## Follow this integration guide to connect your Amazon DSP data to Source Medium.

### Requirements

- **Entity Admin Access** to your Amazon DSP account
- Be able to add Source Medium as a new user and adjust permissions

### **Steps**

To invite a Source Medium to an entity:

1. In the Amazon DSP web interface, select your entity, then select **Entity admin**:

![https://d3a0d0y2hgofx6.cloudfront.net/en-us/_images/dsp/entity-admin.png](https://d3a0d0y2hgofx6.cloudfront.net/en-us/_images/dsp/entity-admin.png)

1. Next, click **Invite user**:

![https://d3a0d0y2hgofx6.cloudfront.net/en-us/_images/dsp/invite-user.png](https://d3a0d0y2hgofx6.cloudfront.net/en-us/_images/dsp/invite-user.png)

1. Enter the Name as Source Medium Team, and the email as integrations@sourcemedium.com. 
Select the Role of Manager
Then, click **Save**:

![](/images/article-imgs/amazon-dsp-integration/Untitled.png)

1. Wait for the success message:

![https://d3a0d0y2hgofx6.cloudfront.net/en-us/_images/dsp/success-message.png](https://d3a0d0y2hgofx6.cloudfront.net/en-us/_images/dsp/success-message.png)

1. Once we are invited as a user with the correct permissions, we will be able to integrate your data into your dashboard!
