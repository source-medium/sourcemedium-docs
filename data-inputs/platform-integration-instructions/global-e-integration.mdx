## Follow this integration guide to connect ****Global-e data in  SourceMedium.****

### Requirements

- Use of Global-e for international commerce within Shopify

### Background

Merchants use Global-e within Shopify to help manage international sales and taxes. Using Global-e however will impact the Gross Sales calculations in our Shopify Sales reports due to the inclusion of duties & taxes. 

SourceMedium has partnered with Global-e to retrieve and separate of the duties & taxes from Gross Sales within our data model and pre-built Looker reports. 

Reach out to your SourceMedium manager to have this feature enabled.
