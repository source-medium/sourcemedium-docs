## Follow this integration guide to connect your Pinterest Ads data to SourceMedium.

### Requirements

- **Admin access** for Pinterest Ads to be able to share permissions to external accounts

### Steps

1. Log into your Pinterest business account 
2. Navigate to top-right corner of your screen > **Business Access** 
    
    ![](/images/article-imgs/pinterest-integration/Untitled.png)
    
3. Click **Partners** in the left-side navigation > **Add partner** to add SourceMedium as a partner on the account 
    1. Enter SourceMedium's partner business ID**: 714242959562018170**
    2. Select **invite a partner to access your assets**
    
    ![](/images/article-imgs/pinterest-integration/Untitled1.png)
    
    ![](/images/article-imgs/pinterest-integration/Untitled2.png)
    
4. Click **Assign ad accounts** to assign permissions, give SourceMedium **Analyst** permission 

***Alternate method to provide access to SourceMedium*** (if prior steps cannot be completed)

1. Email your **Business ID** to integrations@sourcemedium.com
    1. How do I find my Business ID? Visit [https://www.pinterest.com/business/business-access/](https://www.pinterest.com/business/business-access/)
        
        ![](/images/article-imgs/pinterest-integration/Untitled3.png)
        
2. SourceMedium will request access to the account 

### **Additional information**

- If you're having trouble adding SourceMedium as a partner, reference Pinterest's [Manage Partners](https://help.pinterest.com/en/business/article/share-and-manage-access-to-your-ad-accounts) help article
