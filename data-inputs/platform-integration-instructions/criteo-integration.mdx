## Follow this integration guide to connect your Criteo data to SourceMedium.

### Requirements

- **Admin access** to your Criteo account

### **Steps**

1. Log in to and open the account you are connecting with SourceMedium.

2. Navigate to **Settings > Team**.

    ![](/images/article-imgs/criteo-integration/criteo_img_1.png)

3. Select **Add New User**.

    ![](/images/article-imgs/criteo-integration/criteo_img_2.png)


4. Enter the email integrations4@sourcemedium.com and the role of **Administrator**.

    ![](/images/article-imgs/criteo-integration/criteo_img_3.png)

5. Click on **Send Invitation**. 

    ![](/images/article-imgs/criteo-integration/criteo_img_4.png)

Our team will accept the invitation and begin ingesting your Criteo data.
