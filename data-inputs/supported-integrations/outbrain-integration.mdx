## Follow this integration guide to connect your Outbrain data to Source Medium.

### Requirements

- Access to Outbrainâ€™s Amplify API with Owner Role.
- If there is no access to the API, fill out this form [here](https://lp.outbrain.com/partner-api-form/) to request API access for Outbrain.

### Steps

1. Login to Outbrain
2. Click on the **User Management** icon on the left sidebar. After clicking on **User Management**, on the top right of the screen, there will be an **+ Invite Users** button. Click on it.
    
    ![](/images/article-imgs/outbrain-integration/ob_invite1.png)
    
3. In the pop up, fill out the two fields with: 
 
    - **Email Address:** Integrations@sourcemedium.com
    - **Role:** Admin
    
    Click on **Invite** to finish the process.
    
    ![](/images/article-imgs/outbrain-integration/ob_invite2.png)
