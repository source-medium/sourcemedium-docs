## Follow this integration guide to connect your Google Analytics 4 data to Source Medium

### Requirements

- **Admin access** for Google Analytics - [**learn more about Google Analytics roles**](https://support.google.com/analytics/answer/9305587?hl=en)

### Steps

1. Provide access to Google Analytics to Source Medium
    1. Login to your **Google Analytics a**ccount
    2. Click **Admin**, at the bottom left of the page
        
        ![Untitled](/images/article-imgs/ga4-integration/Untitled.png)
        
    3. **Make sure you are in the correct account and GA4 property.**
    4. In the Account column, click **Access Management**.
        
        ![Untitled](/images/article-imgs/ga4-integration/Untitled1.png)
        
    5. In the *Account permissions* list click **+**, then click **Add users**.
        1. Enter  [](https://www.notion.so/ga-sourceMedium-1b424f6a28a64b1292c068cf7a10040c?pvs=21)integrations4@sourcemedium.com as a new user email.dashboards
        2. Select **Notify new users by email** to send a message to the user.
        3. Select the permissions to **Viewer.**
            
            ![Untitled](/images/article-imgs/ga4-integration/Untitled2.png)
            
        4. Click **Add**. 
2. Once Source Medium receives the email notification, we will connect and begin to integrate your GA4 data to your dashboard.
