## Follow this integration guide to connect your Snapchat Ads data to Source Medium.

### **Requirements**

- **Admin access** to your Snapchat Ads Business account to share permissions to external accounts

### **Steps**

1. Provide partner access to Source Medium
    1. Login to your Snapchat Ads Business account and navigate to **Business** section > **Ad Accounts** 
        
        ![](/images/article-imgs/snapchat-integration/Untitled.png)
        
    2. Locate and select your **Ad account > Members and Billing** 
        
        ![](/images/article-imgs/snapchat-integration/Untitled1.png)
        
        ![](/images/article-imgs/snapchat-integration/Untitled2.png)
        
    3. Add integrations@sourcemedium.com as a new member and set the permissions seen in the screenshot below as a **Data Analyst role**. This will send an email to inviting Source Medium to connect your data
        
        ![](/images/article-imgs/snapchat-integration/Untitled3.png)
        

### Additional information

- To help troubleshoot issues relating to adding new members, check the **[Snapchat Manage Members article](https://businesshelp.snapchat.com/s/article/manage-members?language=en_US)**
