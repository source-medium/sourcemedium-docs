## Follow this integration guide to connect your TikTok data to Source Medium.

### Requirements

- **Admin access** to your TikTok Business account

### Steps

1. Invite Source Medium as a member to your TikTok Business account
    1. From your TikTok Business account, navigate to **Users** > **Members** > Select **Invite Member** 
    2. Add **[integrations@sourcemedium.com](mailto:integrations@sourcemedium.com)** as a new member and select **Standard access settings.** This will send an email to inviting Source Medium to connect your data

![](/images/article-imgs/tiktok-integration/Untitled.png)

1. Once you have approved Standard access for Source Medium to your Business Center:
    1. Go to your Business Center and click **Ad Account** under Assets
    2. Select the member (Source Medium) for whom you want to edit permissions to ad account
    3. Toggle the permissions for ad account
        1. Select only the **Analyst** role
    4. Click **Save**.
