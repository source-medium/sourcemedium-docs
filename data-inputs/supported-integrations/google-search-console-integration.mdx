## Follow this integration guide to connect your Google Search Console data to Source Medium.

### Requirements

- **Owner or Full user** access for **Google Search Console** to add users

### Steps

1. Navigate to Google Search Console Settings & Click on **Users and permissions**
    
    ![](/images/article-imgs/google-search-console-integration/Untitled.png)
    
2. Click **ADD USER** on the upper left-hand corner
    
    ![](/images/article-imgs/google-search-console-integration/Untitled1.png)
    
3. Grant Source Medium access by adding **[integrations4@sourcemedium.com**](mailto:integrations4@sourcemedium.com) and set permission to **Restricted**, and click **ADD**
    
    ![](/images/article-imgs/google-search-console-integration/Untitled2.png)
